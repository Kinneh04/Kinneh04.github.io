Some bonus avocado facts

An avocado also contains:

  • 485 milligrams of potassium
  • 81 micrograms of folate
  • 0.257 milligrams of vitamin B6
  • 10 milligrams of vitamin C
  • 2.07 milligrams of vitamin E

Other foods that have the same benefits as avocados include:

  • Various nuts
  • Chia Seeds
  • Hummus
  • Full Fat Yogurt
  • Dark Chocolate
