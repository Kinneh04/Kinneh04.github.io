Avocado Chips

Serving Size Calories / Serving Prep time
15 servings 120 cal 40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 large ripe avocado
  • 3/4 cup of freshly grated Parmesan
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon juice
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of italian seasoning
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground Black Pepper

Description:

Hot tip for getting your chips nice and thin: spray the bottom of a
dry measuring cup with nonstick cooking spray and
press lightly on the scoops of avocado mixture to flatten.

Avocado Egg Boats

Serving Size Calories / Serving Prep time
4 servings 220 cal 40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 large ripe avocados, pitted
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • Finely chopped chives for garnish
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground Black Pepper

Description:

Avocado's creamy texture pairs perfectly with a just
set egg and crispy bacon. It's a power breakfast
move or healthy snack that will keep you moving!

Sinh Tố Bơ

Serving Size Calories / Serving Prep time
1 servings 230 cal 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe Haas avocado
  • 1 cup of ice
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk

Description:

Sinh Tố Bơ (literally “butter fruit smoothie”) is basically a
Vietnamese avocado milkshake. Condensed milk is used to heighten
the naturally sweet and nutty flavors of ripe avocado.
